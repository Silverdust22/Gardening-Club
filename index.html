We are a gardening club located in East London. Our club was formed by five friends who are keen Gardeners. Today we are a 70 member community sharing knowledge and expertise in Gardening.Whether you have an allotment, front/back garden ,a balcony garden or just love plants, we have something for everyone. Join us today and be a member of our blossoming gardening community.
